Frequently Asked Questions

What type of jobs are available?

There are a range of jobs available on Airtasker from tasks around the home like deliveries, cleaning, gardening and handyman work to tasks for businesses like office admin, promotional work or computer & IT support. There also a range of creative tasks like photography, graphic design and website & blog support.

How do I get paid?

There are two payment methods through which you can be paid for completing a task. This can be either cash on delivery or securely via PayPal. If the task is paid by PayPal, you'll need to create a PayPal account - but don't worry, this only takes a few minutes.

Who will I be working with?

It's totally up to you! When browsing jobs, you can check out profiles, reviews and recommendations to determine who you'll be working with.
